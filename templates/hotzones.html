Hot Zones for Flipping in Denver

Using a csv of past real estate investments in Denver, I used gmplot, which generates HTML and javascript to render data on Google Maps, to visualize heat maps of flipping activity in Denver. The gif below shows the evolution of flipping hot zones in Denver over the past 5 years. Each frame of the gif represents four months (either JFMA, MJJA, or SOND). Top right corner of gif shows the time period.
