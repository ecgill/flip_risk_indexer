Hot Zones

The opportunities for real estate investment in Denver is exploding and always changing. Maps and gifs are used to view shifting hot spots of real estate investment through time.

Flip Risk Model

A Kernel Density Estimator was used to quantify these hot zones and use them as features in a Random Forest regression model. The RF regression model uses hot zone metrics plus MLS listing data to predict your ROI given one of three type of flips: fix 'n flip, pop-top, total tear-down.
